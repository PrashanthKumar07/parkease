ABOUT

Parkeasy offers a smart one-stop solution for manual parking. The user is provided with a local wallet exclusively for the application. You can easily update your wallet balance once you log in to your account. As soon as your car enters the parking lot, its license plate is scanned and identified via smart ANPR (Automatic License Plate Recognition) technology. The exit time is recorded similarly. Based on these times, your total parking time is calculated and displayed on your personalized account table once you log in. Your parking time is then multiplied with our base price to generate the aggregate price, which is automatically deducted from your Parkeasy local wallet.

Isn't it literally Park-Easy?!
